## Recommendations

### For Low-End GPUs
1. Use `sim-heat-haze-field` instead of multi-pass fluids
2. Reduce `neural-raymarcher` MAX_STEPS to 48
3. Disable volumetric effects on integrated graphics

### For High-End GPUs
1. Increase ray steps for sharper raymarched edges
2. Enable full FBM octaves in all shaders
3. Use multi-pass shaders at full resolution

### For Mobile/Tablet
1. Target simple shaders: `sim-sand-dunes`, `sim-ink-diffusion`
2. Avoid: `gravitational-lensing`, `neural-raymarcher`
3. Use half-resolution rendering
