# KIMI BRIEF — vhs-tracking-mouse

## Creative Spark
A complete VHS deck degradation simulator: tracking bar follows mouse Y with horizontal wobble, chroma channels bleed horizontally, scanlines drop out randomly, and occasional "tracking loss" white flashes spike on treble hits. The whole image should feel like it's playing from a tape that's been in a basement for 20 years — but the mouse controls exactly where the degradation is worst.

This week we are pushing: **authentic VHS signal degradation + chroma bleed + scanline dropouts + tracking-loss flashes**. Do not produce clean digital glitches or film grain.

## Differentiate From
- `analog-film-degrade` (Copilot today): film dust and scratches — yours is magnetic tape artifacts
- `waveform-glitch` (image): digital block corruption — yours is analog signal degradation
- `vhs-tracking` (if it exists): static tracking — yours is fully interactive with dynamic signal loss

## Wow Mandate
The tracking bar should feel "grabby" — when the mouse is still, the bar jitters slightly. When moved quickly, the whole image should experience horizontal hold wobble and chroma separation. A treble spike should trigger a full-screen white flash + 2–3 frames of severe tracking noise.
