# KIMI BRIEF — concentric-spin

## Creative Spark
Concentric rings that rotate in alternating directions become a chromatic orbital system: each RGB channel orbits at a slightly different radius so the rings shimmer with pure rainbow separation at high speed. Audio bass spawns new rings from the center; treble compresses the gaps into a solid disc of color. Depth shifts the ring center via parallax so the effect feels 3D.

This week we are pushing: **chromatic orbital dispersion + audio-driven geometry + parallax depth**. Do not produce simple color wheels or kaleidoscope symmetry.

- `kaleido-scope-grokcf1` (Kimi A): hexagonal symmetry — yours is concentric, not tiled

## Wow Mandate
